5. Studio Analysis: Does the Studio Impact Anime Ratings?
Objective:
Determine if certain anime studios consistently produce higher-rated anime.

Skills Practiced:

Data Aggregation (grouping by studio)
Statistical Analysis (T-tests, ANOVA)
Visualization (box plots, violin plots)

Potential Insights:

Which studios consistently produce highly ranked anime?
Are big studios (e.g., Madhouse, Kyoto Animation) more successful?
Do smaller studios produce hidden gems?
